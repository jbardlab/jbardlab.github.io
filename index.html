Bard Lab

The Bard Lab in the Department of Biology at Texas A&M University studies the cellular response to stress, with a particular focus on stress-induced translational reprogramming and biomolecular condensation during viral infection and in cancer. We take an integrative approach to address these questions, combining biophysical, biochemical, and high-throughput tools to gain a deep understanding of molecular mechanisms and functions.

This website is still under construction, so stay tuned for more information!
